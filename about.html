Our Story

Built around a feeling.

VW Lifestyle Club started in a parking lot. A handful of cars, a few stories, and the kind of shared obsession that doesn't really go away. Years later we're still here — and so are the people.

We are not a dealership, a forum, or a fan page. We are a community. South African, Volkswagen-rooted, lifestyle-first.

Vision

An automotive lifestyle, written in our own voice.

To be the home of South African Volkswagen culture — confident, modern and rooted in the streets.

Mission

Bring the community together — on the road and off it.

Build events, stories and partnerships that celebrate the cars and the people behind them.

Culture

Craft, respect and the long drive.

We value clean builds, generous hosts, and the kind of weekends you remember years later.

Lifestyle Focus

The drive is the start. Not the end.

Coffee runs, sunset linkups, weekend convoys, build sessions — the lifestyle lives between the events.
